# Contact

Are you interested in livecoding? Do you have an idea for a performance, collaboration, or any questions and suggestions? We have a thriving community & the best way to stay in touch is by joining our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/rxK7vF6PVW) or following us on [instagram](https://www.instagram.com/livecode.blr/). 

You can also join our members directory by following the instructions in the README to "Add yourself" and become a part of the community, as well as make yourself discoverable to other creative coding practitioners across the country!
